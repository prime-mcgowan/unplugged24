Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the unplugged retreat?

Unplug and unwind as soon as you arrive. Our retreat is conveniently located 45 minutes outside of St. Paul and Minneapolis. For your comfort, we offer complimentary pickup services. If you prefer to drive, we can provide detailed driving directions and recommendations for rest stops along the way. Let us handle the logistics so you can focus on relaxation and rejuvenation.

What kind of team is right for the unplugged?

Unplugged is ideal for teams seeking rejuvenation and stronger bonds. Tech-dependent teams, those feeling burned out, or aiming for innovation will benefit. Our retreat fosters creativity, improves communication, and builds company culture. Whether you need to boost morale or problem-solve, Unplugged offers the perfect escape for enhanced teamwork.

What size teams can you accomadate?

Unplugged can accommodate a variety of team sizes. Small teams of 5-15 members find our intimate setting ideal for focused collaboration. Medium-sized teams of 15-30 can enjoy a range of activities and social events. Larger groups of 30+ can be accommodated with careful planning, potentially requiring multiple spaces or breakout sessions. To determine the exact capacity, factors like meeting space size, accommodation options, and desired activities must be considered.

Is there really no wi-fi available?

We believe that fully immersing yourself in the Unplugged experience is key to its benefits. That's why we've created a Wi-Fi-free zone to encourage connection and creativity. We understand that in today's world, it's easy to feel tied to technology, but we promise it's worth unplugging! Of course, we realize that unexpected things happen. If you have a true emergency, we can make accommodations, but we want to emphasize that our focus is on providing a completely unplugged environment.
